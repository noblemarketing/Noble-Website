Signature

Tier 01 · Squarespace or Shopify · 2–3 weeks

Signature is built for brands that aren't ready to invest in a fully custom site but still want a real online home—not a generic template with a logo dropped in. We translate your existing branding into a cohesive, brand-centric website on Squarespace or Shopify, using the platform's structure so you get polish without custom-built complexity.

You launch with a site that feels personal and intentional, then take the reins: straightforward handoff, manageable day-to-day updates, and a walkthrough so you can run it yourself after go-live.

What's included
  • Up to 5 pages, fully designed to your brand
  • Customized website with easy handoff for you to manage the site post-project
  • Mobile and desktop optimization across devices
  • SEO foundations (meta titles, descriptions, image alt text)
  • Contact form setup and integrations
  • 1-hour walkthrough so you can manage your site with confidence after launch

Best for: Teams who want a brand-forward site, a lighter investment than full custom work, and the confidence to manage their own content after launch.

Custom

Tier 02 · Custom coded · 4–6 weeks

For the business that wants a truly custom website without the complexity of a ground-up build. The Custom package is built on a refined, battle-tested framework—proven page architecture and layout logic that we fully skin, customize, and build out to your brand.

You get the speed and reliability of a proven foundation with the look and feel of something built just for you. Clean code, thoughtful design, and a handoff that actually makes sense.

What's included
  • Up to 10 pages with unique custom design
  • Proven framework foundation, fully built to your brand
  • SEO foundations (meta titles, descriptions, image alt text)
  • Third-party integrations (booking, email, CRM)
  • Mobile and desktop optimization across devices
  • 2 weeks of post-launch support

Best for: Businesses ready to invest in a custom-coded site that looks intentional, works reliably, and is built to grow with them.

Premium

Tier 03 · Fully custom build · 6–10 weeks

The most elevated offering Noble provides. The Premium package is a fully bespoke website built from the ground up—custom-coded, deeply strategic, and engineered for performance. No platform constraints. No template starting points. Just a purpose-built digital experience designed around your business goals.

Every Premium project begins with a dedicated strategy session to map your user journey, content architecture, and conversion goals before a single pixel is designed.

What's included
  • Full strategy and discovery session
  • Unlimited pages—scoped to your project
  • Custom development (React / fully bespoke stack)
  • Advanced integrations and API connections
  • E-commerce capability (if needed)
  • Performance and Core Web Vitals optimization
  • Mobile and desktop optimization across devices
  • Full SEO architecture from the ground up
  • 30 days of post-launch support

Best for: Established businesses, rebrands, e-commerce, and anyone who wants their website to be a genuine competitive advantage.
