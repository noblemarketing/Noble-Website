Clarity, craft, and strategy—for brands ready to grow

As a Graphic Designer and Strategic Marketer, Audrey brings a diverse skill set and a deep passion for helping businesses elevate their brands with clarity and intention. Her expertise spans brand identity development, creative direction, and digital marketing, where she focuses on building visually compelling solutions that are not only aesthetically refined, but strategically effective.

With hands-on experience across social media platforms, website design and development, and multi-channel marketing, Audrey has worked with businesses in ecommerce, professional services, manufacturing, outdoor and lifestyle brands, hospitality, and creative industries. Her work includes designing and managing social media content, developing conversion-focused websites, and crafting cohesive brand systems that translate seamlessly across digital and print touchpoints.

Driven by a commitment to excellence, Audrey is dedicated to exceeding client expectations—creating work that not only looks polished, but performs with purpose.

OUR PROCESS

From discovery call to finished brand
— in the right order.

  1. STEP 01

    Discovery call

    We start with a conversation about your goals, audience, and where your brand is today—so the work ahead is aligned before anything is designed or built.

  2. STEP 02

    Design and Build

    Logo, identity, website, and supporting assets take shape from your brief—crafted with intention and built to work across every channel you rely on.

  3. STEP 03

    Refinement

    We collaborate on feedback, tighten details, and polish until the work feels right—not just finished—so what goes live reflects your brand with clarity.

  4. STEP 04

    Launch and Elevate

    Launch with confidence: SEO, Google Business, social, and aligned messaging so your new brand is visible, consistent, and ready for the audience you want to reach.

Remote collaborations

Many of Noble's projects run fully remotely—clear communication, shared tools, and a steady rhythm so distance never gets in the way of the work.

From first discovery call through brand guidelines, website launch, and social content, collaboration happens over video, email, and shared workspaces. You'll see concepts on a predictable schedule, have space to gather feedback from your team, and get files and handoff notes organized so nothing gets lost between time zones.
