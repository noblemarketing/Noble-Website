Boutique Branding, Built With Care

A Pennsylvania studio partnering with US brands to refine identity, digital presence, and how you show up on social—without the corporate conveyor belt.

Meet the studio & founder

Founded by Audrey, Noble is a small-by-design brand studio: fewer concurrent projects, more thoughtful iteration, and direct access to the person making the calls.

We work with founders, marketing leads, and internal teams to translate personality into a cohesive visual story—from logo and packaging cues to a website that finally feels on-brand, plus social that breathes the same air.

Most engagements blend strategy and craft across Pennsylvania and remote collaborations nationwide; where you are matters less than how clearly you can share what makes your brand different.

South Central & Central Pennsylvania

The studio is located in South Central Pennsylvania. We regularly partner with organizations throughout the region, including in Adams, Cumberland, Dauphin, Franklin, Lancaster, Lebanon, Perry, and York counties, and in towns and cities such as Harrisburg, Lancaster, York, Carlisle, Chambersburg, Gettysburg, Lebanon, Hershey, and Mechanicsburg. Local meetings are welcome when it fits your project; many milestones happen remotely with the same clarity and care.

Virginia, North Carolina, Florida & Beyond

Noble has also supported clients outside Pennsylvania, including work tied to Lynchburg, Virginia; to Raleigh, Charlotte, and Greenville, North Carolina; and to partners and projects in Florida—plus ongoing remote collaborations nationwide. Wherever you are, the process stays organized: discovery, strategy, design, and launch with timelines you can plan around.

Industries & organizations we partner with

Noble Marketing & Design works with small businesses, nonprofits, schools, churches, hospitality brands, and professional services teams—anywhere you need branding, website design, or social media that feels cohesive and credible.

Remote collaborations

Many of Noble's projects run fully remotely—clear communication, shared tools, and a steady rhythm so distance never gets in the way of the work.

From first discovery call through brand guidelines, website launch, and social content, collaboration happens over video, email, and shared workspaces. You'll see concepts on a predictable schedule, have space to gather feedback from your team, and get files and handoff notes organized so nothing gets lost between time zones.
