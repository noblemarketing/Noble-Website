Noble Marketing Portfolio — Client Profiles

1. Brookstone Industries
Type of Service: Branding + Website Niche: Metal Fabrication / Industrial Manufacturing Location: PA Short Description: Precision sheet metal ductwork and custom metal fabrication company serving industrial, commercial, and residential projects. Known for their "Better Metal Faster" motto, they specialize in HVAC duct fabrication and custom fittings out of Ephrata, PA.
The Challenge
Brookstone Industries had the craftsmanship and the track record, but their brand and online presence didn’t reflect it. Their existing logo felt dated and failed to communicate the precision and professionalism behind their work. Without a strong visual identity or a website built to convert, they were losing credibility with potential commercial and industrial clients who were evaluating them against more polished competitors. They needed a brand that could hold its own in a highly technical field — one that communicated their “Better Metal Faster” promise at a glance.
The Solution
Noble developed a complete brand identity for Brookstone, starting with a new logo and visual system built around themes of precision, reliability, and industrial craftsmanship. The brand voice was defined around their core strengths: experience, speed, and quality. Noble then designed and built a full website structured around clear user pathways — service discovery, industry-specific pages, and contact/quote conversion. Testimonials, experience milestones, and service highlights were woven throughout to reinforce trust and authority with decision-makers in commercial and industrial fields. The result is a responsive, professional site that works for field professionals and project managers alike.
