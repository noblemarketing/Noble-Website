The challenge

Lakewood Reserve was launching a new short-term rental experience — a modern lakeside tiny home retreat on Raystown Lake — and needed to build an entire brand presence from scratch. In a growing glamping and boutique hospitality market, they needed a brand identity, a website, and social media management that could communicate the elevated, immersive quality of the experience and attract guests looking for something more than a standard vacation rental.

The solution

Noble handled full brand development, website design, and ongoing social media management for Lakewood Reserve. The brand was built around the retreat’s core appeal — elevated comfort surrounded by woods, water, and mountains — with a visual identity that feels both modern and grounded in nature. The website and social media presence work together to showcase the experience, build anticipation, and drive bookings, giving Lakewood Reserve a polished, cohesive presence across every touchpoint.
